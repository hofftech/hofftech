Similar to the Field of Heroes, the Healing Field represents the 3113 people that died during the attacks on 9/11. We built a QR-based app so that visitors could learn more about them.

Idaho Field of Heroes

Every Memorial Day, 6827 crosses are set up in Pocatello Idaho, one for each soldier that died in the Middle East. We built a QR-based app so that visitors could learn more about them.
